This series has one of the best story mode

Luffy is crazy and challenges all the powerful guys and engage in a physical fights, he is so carefree even he is a wanted man he just do not care what others think, he loves meat food a lot and his aim is to become King of the Pirates. He and his team mates gets on a ship and start to go on a journey so they can find the one piece treasure in the big sea, whoever finds this treasure will become the king of the pirates.

There are amazing adventures lies ahead in journey where luffy makes lot of friends and many enemies.
